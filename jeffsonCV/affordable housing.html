Millimani Housing Project

Millimani Project: Kilizoni Luxury Apartments

Project Overview

Our housing project consists of luxury apartments located in a prime area, offering high-end features and amenities. The total cost for each apartment is Ksh 1 million. Below is a breakdown of the costs and luxury features included:


Cost Breakdown

  1. Construction Cost: Ksh 600,000
    • Includes materials, labor, and construction permits.
  2. Interior Design: Ksh 150,000
    • High-quality finishes, fixtures, and fittings.
  3. Land Cost: Ksh 100,000
    • Cost of acquiring the land.
  4. Utilities: Ksh 50,000
    • Installation of water, electricity, and sewerage systems.
  5. Miscellaneous: Ksh 100,000
    • Contingency fund and other miscellaneous expenses.

Luxury Features

  1. Spacious Layout: Each apartment offers a spacious living area, bedrooms, and bathrooms.
  2. Modern Kitchen: Fully equipped modern kitchen with high-quality appliances and fittings.
  3. Luxurious Bathrooms: Stylish bathrooms with modern fixtures and fittings, including a bathtub and shower.
  4. Balcony: Each apartment has a private balcony offering panoramic views of the surrounding area.
  5. Security: 24/7 security surveillance, secure parking, and access control systems.
  6. Green Spaces: Landscaped gardens and recreational areas for residents to enjoy.
  7. Smart Home Technology: Integration of smart home features for convenience and energy efficiency.
  8. High-Speed Internet: Fiber optic internet connectivity for residents.
  9. Fitness Center: Fully equipped fitness center for residents to stay active and healthy.
  10. wimming Pool: Relaxing swimming pool area with sun loungers and a poolside bar.
